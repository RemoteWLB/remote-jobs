# **Job Details:**

## Job Description:

Intel Labs is seeking motivated graduate interns for a virtual internship conducting research on knowledge retrieval systems and language models. This is a temporary position working with research mentors in the Brain-Inspired Computing Lab (BCL) located in Santa Clara, California, and Hillsboro, Oregon.  
  
At BCL, you will work with other Intel teams and researchers around the world to develop new forms of artificial intelligence and to study the best underlying computing technologies for it. You will research, experiment with, and prototype ideas that impact Intel and its customers.  
  
During your internship, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and evaluating new artificial intelligence algorithms and systems inspired by neuroscience and the cognitive sciences. This requires some familiarity with techniques in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and relevant software frameworks. Experience in neuroscience and the cognitive sciences is not required, but interest in them will benefit you. You will work in a team-oriented collaborative environment to meet your goals. You are expected to utilize state-of-the-art techniques, form strong relationships with researchers and key collaborators, and contribute to Intel technologies and/or the scientific literature. You will be expected to focus on a specific project of mutual interest to you and your mentor(s), focusing on a key research question that will move your work forward.
