Develop, implement, and manage QA processes to ensure that Salesforce products meet required specifications for quality, function, and reliability prior to delivery. Work with product owners, developers, internal and external stakeholders, and business owners in all phases of the Salesforce development cycle offering QA assistance and QA process optimization regarding Salesforce platform. Ensure and record requirement coverage by verification. Manage Salesforce Testing and oversee Salesforce CRM UI and backend web-services Functional, Integration, End to End Testing, and Support UAT as Senior Software Engineer in the Agile team. Enable production
support and automation of features suitable for test automation with Selenium and Postman. Test user Stories developed by the development team in an optimal Agile development environment. Govern and direct testing best practices, test methodologies, and test delivery for Salesforce systems. Identify, understand, document, and create functionality that aligns with product and capability definitions laid out by product managers. Develop and design testing program strategy for Salesforce systems. Plan, conduct, and monitor the test, and inspect Salesforce products to ensure quality. Develop integration testing strategies to test data flow between Salesforce and ERP Systems. Assist QA team with quality and availability of Salesforce test environment. Ensure features and stories are met with acceptance criteria by developing effective test strategy. Enhance QA methodology for test and defect management and tools supporting processes. Develop and monitor corrective measures. Manage external

Lead defect triage meetings, escalate, and see defects to closure within timelines. Maintain JIRA for defect management activities. Track customer-facing issues in an agile tracking tool. Escalate unresolved issues to respective teams and bring them to closure. Train in Salesforce testing, specialized knowledge in test estimation, test planning, and test automation. Utilize testing tools, postman, SoapUI, and selenium. Work with software programming languages, SQL/SOQL, Java, JavaScript, XML, and
