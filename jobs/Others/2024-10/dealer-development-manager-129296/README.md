#### ` remote,Work From Home`  

## Description

The work we do has an impact on millions of lives, and you can be a part of it.

We help protect our customers against lifes uncertainties. Regardless of where you work within the company, youll be helping provide protection and peace of mind when our customers need it most.

  

The Dealer Development Manager is responsible for providing sales and service to a network of dealerships for our Specialty division which includes powersports, RV, and marine product portfolios. This includes maintaining and developing existing dealer relationships within the Specialty division as well as acquisitions of new client accounts.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

路 Develop and execute a strategy for increasing overall product mix within existing customers while building and maintaining relationships with key personnel

路 Utilize CRM to actively track and analyze sales leads and customer interactions to enhance business relationships with customers and drive sales growth

路 Establish ongoing communication with dealers to create visibility before, during and after every client visit

路 Capable of identifying customer challenges and providing solutions in a manner ultimately leading to trusted advisor status

路 Utilize consultative assessment skills to optimize dealer performance

路 Leverage customer relationships to generate leads and acquisition appointments

路 Maintaining industry knowledge surrounding trends and company initiatives

COMMUNICATIONS:

This position is in continuous contact with external and internal customers. As an ambassador of our company and brand, this position requires strong presentation skills and communication skills.

PROFESSIONAL SPECIFICATIONS:

路 Bachelors degree preferred but not required

路 2-5 years powersports, RV, or marine industry experience preferred

REQUIREMENTS:

路 Strong focus on customer service, which includes excellent listening skills, verbal and written communication skills as well as interpersonal skills

路 Microsoft Office (in particular, Excel and Power Point)

路 Demonstrates ability to collaborate with others

路 Team player capable of building strong lasting business relationships

路 Must have a proven track record of developing sales results in an assigned territory.

路 Possess an organized approach to problem solving

路 Must own a vehicle and have good driving record

Must be able to travel up to 75% of the time by air or car
