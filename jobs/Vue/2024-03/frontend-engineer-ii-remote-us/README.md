**Job Details**

We're hiring a **Frontend Engineer II** to join our Engineering team and build user interfaces for Openly's insurance platform. You will play a critical role in designing, building, testing, and maintaining our user experiences and contribute to the overall technology ecosystem that power our insurance products.

 **Key Responsibilities**

  * Enhance the web interface that agents and internal team members use to access our insurance platform
  * Work to reduce complexity and simplify technical design
  * Identify risks and opportunities and create solutions with the team
  * Implement design/UX initiatives through collaboration with our Product & Strategy team
  * Develop interfaces for internal users to administer agencies, agents, and policies
  * Work with the team to help deliver on the overall technical and architectural vision
  * Nurture relationships within the team to identify and improve internal processes
  * Collaborate with internal partners to leverage APIs that power our best-in-class user experiences

 **Requirements**

  * 2+ years of professional/production experience developing web applications
  * Ability to explain technical challenges to both technical and non-technical audiences
  * Professional/production experience with Vue.js * _ **Please note that part of the interview process includes completing a code challenge in Vue.**_
  * Proficiency in modern JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3
  * Keep up with major frontend & UX design trends & their implementations
  * Understanding of REST & GraphQL API integrations
  * Excellent communication skills
  * Familiarity with Unit Testing

 _If you don't think you meet all of the criteria below but still are interested in the job, please apply. Nobody checks every box, and we're looking for someone excited to join the team._

 **Our Tools**

  * Backend/Core: Go & Postgresql
  * Frontend: Browser-based, VueJS, Webpack, Nuxt & Tailwind
  * Research/Data Science: R, ArcGIS, Jupyter Notebooks, & Python
  * Infrastructure: Google Cloud, specifically Cloud Run, Kubernetes, Pub/Sub, BigQuery, and CloudSQL, managed with Terraform. We use GitHub for code hosting, DataDog and HoneyComb for monitoring, and CircleCI for running our CI/CD pipelines
  * Remote work tools: Slack, Zoom, Donut
